Central Texas Public Meeting Calendars

Subscribable calendars of Central Texas public meetings, rebuilt daily from official sources. "Subscribe" adds a live feed to your calendar app; "Download" grabs a one-time snapshot.

Tip: in Apple Calendar, set the subscription's Auto-refresh to "Every day" (the default is weekly) so schedule changes reach you promptly. Google Calendar refreshes on its own schedule, roughly daily.
